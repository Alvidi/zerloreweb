# Siege

Siege Mode is an asymmetric format where one player defends a fortified position while the other attempts to conquer it. Ideal for narrative and cinematic games.

## Roles

Before starting, players agree on who is the **Attacker** and who is the **Defender**.

## Army Value

Players freely agree on the total Value for the game. The Defender builds their army with **25% less Value** than the Attacker to compensate for the defensive advantage.

**Example:**
- Value 100 game → Attacker 100, Defender 75
- Value 200 game → Attacker 200, Defender 150

## Setup

1. The Defender chooses their deployment zone — a table edge with defensive scenery such as barricades and turrets is recommended.
2. The Defender places **2 objectives** in their zone. Each objective must be at least 9" from the other and at least 3" long.
3. The Defender deploys their units, turrets, and barricades.
4. The Attacker deploys on the opposite edge.

## Barricades

The Defender may place any piece of scenery as a barricade. Barricades grant **partial cover** to units in base contact with them and cannot be destroyed.

## Turrets

The Defender may include turrets in their list as normal units. They activate like any other unit during the Activation Phase. Two types are available:

## Duration

The game lasts a minimum of 7 turns, always an odd number. Players may agree on more turns before starting.

## Victory

- **The Attacker wins** if they capture both objectives before the turns run out.
- **The Defender wins** if they hold at least 1 objective at the end of the last turn.
