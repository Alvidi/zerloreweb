# Conquest

Conquest Mode is the standard format of ZeroLore. Two players compete for control of the battlefield by occupying strategic zones and destroying enemy units.

## Setup

1. Players agree on army size.
2. Place objectives on the table. Always an odd number, with a minimum of 3. Each objective must be at least 3" long. Size, shape, and position are free as long as both players agree.
3. Deploy following the standard rules.

## Duration

The game lasts a minimum of 5 turns. Players agree on the exact number before starting.

## Scoring

### Objective Control

At the end of each turn, each controlled objective grants **5 points** to the player who controls it. This value is recommended, though players may agree on a different amount before starting.

### Unit Elimination

Destroying an enemy unit earns the following points:

| Type                  | Points |
|-----------------------|--------|
| Line                  | 2      |
| Elite                 | 4      |
| Vehicle / Monster     | 6      |
| Hero                  | 8      |
| Titan                 | 10     |

## Victory

The player with the most points at the end of the last turn wins. In case of a tie, the player who controls the most objectives at that moment wins.
