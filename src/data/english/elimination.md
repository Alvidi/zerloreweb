# Elimination

Elimination Mode is a pure combat format. There are no objectives to capture — only the destruction of the enemy army. Ideal for direct and aggressive games.

## Setup

1. Players agree on army size.
2. Deploy following the standard rules.
3. No objectives are placed on the table.

## Duration

The game lasts a minimum of 5 turns, always an odd number. Players agree on the exact number before starting.

## Scoring

Destroying an enemy unit earns the following points. Each kill counts regardless of whether the unit revives afterwards:

| Type                  | Points |
|-----------------------|--------|
| Line                  | 2      |
| Elite                 | 4      |
| Vehicle / Monster     | 6      |
| Hero                  | 8      |
| Titan                 | 10     |

## Victory

The player with the most points at the end of the last turn wins. In case of a tie, the player who has destroyed the most units in total wins.
